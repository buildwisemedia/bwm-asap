Everything You Need to Get Wildlife Out — and Keep It Out

From emergency removal to full exclusion and disinfection. Every service follows our four-step process so the problem is solved permanently.

🦍

Wildlife Removal

Humane trapping and removal of raccoons, squirrels, bats, snakes, opossums, birds, and more. We identify the animal, understand its behavior, and remove it without poisons or cruelty. Most of our clients call because a previous company trapped and left — we do the full four steps.

Pest Control

Eco-friendly pest control for rodents, insects, and general household pests. Safe methods that protect your family, your pets, and the environment. We address the source of the problem, not just the symptoms — so pests don't keep coming back.

Learn more →
🛡

Exclusion & Sealing

The step most companies skip. We identify and seal every entry point — soffit gaps, roof returns, pipe penetrations, gable vents, and foundation cracks. Backed by our exclusion warranty. If an animal gets back in through a sealed entry, we come back at no charge.

Learn more →
✨

Disinfection & Cleanup

After removal, we clean and disinfect attics, crawl spaces, and vents. We remove droppings, urine, and nesting materials that carry health risks like raccoon roundworm, bat histoplasmosis, and rodent-borne hantavirus. Your space is safe when we're done.

Dead Animal Removal

If something died in your walls, attic, or yard, we remove it, dispose of it properly, and eliminate the odor. Fast response — because every day you wait makes the smell worse and the health risk greater. We handle everything so you don't have to.
