How the Peace-of-Mind Wildlife Shield Works

Four steps. One complete solution. Every step matters — skip one, and the problem comes back.

1

Inspect

A licensed technician comes to your property and conducts a thorough inspection. We identify the animal species, locate every entry point, assess the damage, and check for health hazards from droppings or nesting materials.

What you experience: We walk you through everything we find. You'll know exactly what animal is in your home, how it got in, and what the risks are. No guessing, no surprises.

Behind the scenes: We document every entry point and potential weak spot. This becomes the blueprint for our exclusion work — so nothing gets missed.

🔍

Our inspection fee is credited toward your service if you proceed. Your inspection isn't wasted money — it goes toward the fix.

2

Trap

We position humane traps based on the animal's movement patterns — not just where it's convenient. We monitor the traps regularly until the animals are removed.

What you experience: We explain exactly where the traps are placed, why, and how often we'll check them. You know what to expect at every step.

Behind the scenes: Our methods are approved by the Georgia DNR. No poisons, no cruelty. We use humane, eco-friendly trapping that protects your family, your pets, and the animals.

🪄

This is where most companies stop. They trap, send a bill, and leave. But trapping alone doesn't solve the problem.

3

Exclude

This is the step most companies skip — and it's why animals keep coming back.

We seal every entry point and potential weak spot. Soffit gaps, roof returns, pipe penetrations, gable vents, foundation cracks — everything. We don't just patch the obvious hole. We reinforce your entire roofline and perimeter.

What you experience: We show you every area we sealed and explain why each one matters. You get multiple price options for the repair work — good, better, and best — so you choose what fits your budget.

Behind the scenes: We back our exclusion work with a warranty. If an animal gets back in through a sealed entry point, we come back.

🛡

The #1 reason animals come back: nobody sealed the entry points.

Our exclusion work is warranty-backed.

4

Disinfect

After the animals are gone and the entries are sealed, we clean and disinfect the affected areas. Attics, crawl spaces, vents — we remove droppings, urine, and nesting materials.

What you experience: Your space is clean, safe, and free of health hazards. No more worrying about what's in the insulation above your family's heads.

Behind the scenes: Raccoon droppings carry roundworm. Bat guano causes histoplasmosis. Rodent waste spreads hantavirus. These are real health risks — and our disinfection removes them.

✨

Your home isn't just wildlife-free. It's clean, safe, and protected.
