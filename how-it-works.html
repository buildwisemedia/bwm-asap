Four Steps to a Wildlife-Free Home

Every job follows the same proven process. No shortcuts. No skipped steps.

1

Inspect

We start with a full property inspection. Our licensed technician checks your attic, crawl space, roofline, vents, and foundation. We identify the animal, find every entry point, assess the damage, and check for health hazards. You'll know exactly what's going on before we do anything.

2

Trap

We set traps based on the animal's movement patterns. We monitor them regularly — not once a week. Humane traps only. No poisons, no kill traps. We're licensed by the DNR and follow Georgia law for every species.

3

Exclude

This is the step most companies skip. We seal every entry point and every weak spot. Roof returns, soffit gaps, gable vents, pipe penetrations — all of it. This is why the animals don't come back. We warranty our exclusion work.

4

Disinfect

Animals leave more than damage. Droppings carry raccoon roundworm. Bat guano causes histoplasmosis. Rodent waste spreads hantavirus. We clean and disinfect affected areas — attics, crawl spaces, vents — so your home is safe again.
