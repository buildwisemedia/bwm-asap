How the Peace-of-Mind Wildlife Shield Works

Four steps. One complete solution. No shortcuts.

The Problem with Most Companies

Most wildlife companies stop at step two. They trap the animal and leave. No one seals the entry points. No one cleans up what was left behind. Six months later, the animals are back — and you're paying again.

ASAP does it differently. Our four-step process solves the entire problem.

Our 4-Step Process

1

INSPECT

A licensed technician inspects your property. We find the animal, identify every entry point, assess the damage, and check for health risks.

You'll know exactly what you're dealing with — and exactly what needs to happen.

2

TRAP

We set humane traps based on the animal's movement patterns. We monitor and return regularly until every animal is safely removed.

No poisons. No cruelty. Just effective, humane trapping.

3

EXCLUDE

This is the step most companies skip. We seal every entry point and reinforce weak spots with professional-grade materials.

If we sealed it and something gets back in, we come back. That's our warranty.

4

DISINFECT

Animal droppings, urine, and nesting materials carry diseases. We clean and disinfect attics, crawl spaces, and vents so your home is safe again.

Raccoon roundworm. Bat histoplasmosis. Rodent hantavirus. We remove the risk.
