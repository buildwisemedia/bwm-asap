Our Story

Why ASAP Exists

ASAP Pest and Wildlife started because too many homeowners in Metro Atlanta were getting half the job done. Companies would trap an animal and leave. No sealing. No cleanup. No explanation. Six months later, the animals were back and the homeowner was right where they started.

We built ASAP around a simple idea: solve the whole problem, not just the visible part. That means inspecting to find every entry point. Trapping humanely. Sealing so nothing gets back in. And disinfecting so your home is safe again.

We're family-run. We treat your home like our own. And we're available 24 hours a day, 6 days a week — because wildlife emergencies don't follow a 9-to-5 schedule.

What We Stand For

Humane Treatment

No poisons. No cruelty. We use eco-friendly methods approved by the Georgia DNR. Every animal is handled with care.

Complete Solutions

We don't stop at trapping. Inspect, trap, exclude, disinfect — every step, every time.

Honest Communication

We explain what we find, what it means, and what your options are. Multiple price points so you choose what fits.

Accountability

We warranty our exclusion work. If an animal gets back in through a sealed entry point, we come back.
