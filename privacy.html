Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

ASAP Pest and Wildlife Removal ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at wildliferemovalasap.com or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and property address when you request a quote, book an inspection, or contact us.
  • Scheduling Information: Appointment preferences and details provided through our booking system.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time on site, and referring URL collected automatically when you browse our website.
  • Location Data: General geographic location inferred from your IP address to confirm service area eligibility.
  • Communications: Records of phone calls, emails, text messages, and form submissions you send to us.

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and provide pest control and wildlife removal services
  • To communicate with you about appointments, follow-ups, and service updates
  • To respond to your inquiries and customer service requests
  • To send service reminders and seasonal pest prevention tips (with your consent)
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and gather usage data. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some website functionality.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device until deleted or expired).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Cal.com: Powers our online booking system. When you schedule an inspection, Cal.com processes your name, email, phone number, and appointment details. See Cal.com's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics (GA4): Collects anonymous website usage data including pages visited, session duration, and traffic sources. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Pixel: Tracks website conversions and enables targeted advertising on Facebook and Instagram. See Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Records anonymized session replays and heatmaps to help us understand how visitors use our site. See Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
