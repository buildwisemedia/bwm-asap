How We Handle Wildlife Removal

Step 1

Identify the Species

We inspect your property top to bottom. We look at droppings, entry points, damage patterns, and tracks. This tells us exactly what we're dealing with.

Step 2

Set Humane Traps

We place species-specific live traps in strategic locations. No poison. No glue boards. Just safe, humane trapping that follows Georgia DNR rules.

Step 3

Monitor Regularly

We check traps on a set schedule. We don't leave animals sitting for days. Regular monitoring means faster results and less stress on the animal.

Step 4

Remove and Relocate

Captured animals are safely relocated to approved areas away from your neighborhood. We follow all state and local guidelines for relocation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does wildlife removal cost? +

Wildlife removal typically costs between $200 and $2,000 depending on the species, number of animals, and scope of work. Every job starts with a free inspection so we can give you an accurate quote.

How long does wildlife removal take? +

Most wildlife removal jobs take 3 to 7 days. Some situations like bat colonies may take longer due to state regulations. We monitor traps regularly and keep you updated throughout the process.

What species do you remove? +

We remove raccoons, squirrels, bats, snakes, opossums, birds, and other nuisance wildlife common to Metro Atlanta. We are licensed by Georgia DNR to handle all protected species.

Are your methods humane? +

Yes. We use humane live traps and follow all Georgia DNR guidelines. Animals are safely relocated to approved areas away from residential neighborhoods.

Do you offer a warranty on wildlife removal? +

Yes. When you combine removal with our exclusion service, we warranty the sealed entry points. If animals get back in through our work, we come back at no charge.
