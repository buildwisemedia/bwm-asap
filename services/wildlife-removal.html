The Problem

Wildlife in Your Home Is More Than a Nuisance

Animals in your attic chew wires, tear insulation, and leave droppings that carry health risks like raccoon roundworm, bat histoplasmosis, and rodent-borne hantavirus. Every day they're in your home, the damage gets worse and the cleanup costs more.

And if someone trapped the animals without sealing the entry points — they're coming back. A pair of squirrels in spring becomes a family of 8 by summer. Rats multiply even faster.

Common species we remove:
