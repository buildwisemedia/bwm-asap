Discreet, professional service for apartments, offices, restaurants, and retail properties.

Pest Problems in a Business Can't Wait

Pest problems in a business setting need to be handled quietly and professionally. A rodent sighting at a restaurant can mean a failed health inspection. A wildlife issue at an apartment complex means angry tenants and liability.

You need a pest control company that shows up on time, works around your schedule, keeps a low profile, and gives you documentation for your records.

If this sounds familiar, you need a partner — not just a vendor.

How We Handle Commercial Properties

Step 1

Assessment

We walk your entire property and identify current pest issues, risk areas, and conditions that could lead to problems. We note everything and share it with you.

Step 2

Custom Plan

Every property is different. We build a plan based on your specific needs — the type of business, the layout, the pests involved, and your schedule.

Step 3

Scheduled Service

We come on a regular schedule — monthly, quarterly, or whatever your property needs. We work around your hours so there's no disruption to your business.

Step 4

Documentation and Reporting

After every visit, you get a detailed report. What we found, what we treated, and what we recommend. This keeps you compliant and informed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is commercial pest control different from residential? +

Commercial pest control requires more documentation, compliance with health codes, and discretion. We work around your business hours, use unmarked vehicles when needed, and provide detailed service reports for your records.

Do you offer service contracts? +

Yes. We offer monthly, quarterly, and custom service contracts for commercial properties. Contracts include scheduled visits, emergency service, documentation, and priority response times.

What documentation do you provide? +

We provide detailed service reports after every visit including findings, treatments applied, and recommendations. These reports help you stay compliant with health inspections and building management requirements.

Can you work discreetly? +

Absolutely. We understand that pest problems at a business need to be handled quietly. We can use unmarked vehicles, work during off-hours, and keep our presence low-profile to protect your reputation.

Do you offer emergency commercial service? +

Yes. Contract customers get priority emergency response. We understand that a pest issue at a restaurant or apartment complex can't wait until next week. Call us and we'll be there fast.
