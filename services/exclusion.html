Frequently Asked Questions

What is wildlife exclusion?
Wildlife exclusion is the process of sealing every entry point that animals use to get into your home. This includes gaps in the roofline, soffit openings, foundation cracks, vent covers, and plumbing penetrations. Exclusion is the only way to make sure animals don't come back after removal.
Do you warranty your exclusion work?
Yes, we warranty our exclusion work. If animals get back in through any area we sealed, we come back and fix it at no extra cost. Our warranty gives you peace of mind that the job was done right and will hold up over time.
How do animals get into my attic?
Animals get in through gaps in the roofline, damaged soffit panels, open vents, construction gaps, and even plumbing penetrations. A squirrel only needs a gap the size of a golf ball. A mouse can squeeze through a hole the size of a dime. We find openings you'd never notice.
What materials do you use to seal entry points?
We use professional-grade materials including galvanized steel mesh, metal flashing, commercial-grade sealants, and hardware cloth. These materials are chew-proof and weather-resistant. We match the materials to the animal species and the location of the entry point.
