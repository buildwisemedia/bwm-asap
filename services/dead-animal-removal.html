Dead Animal Removal — Fast, Clean, Done Right

Strange smell in your home? An animal may have died in your walls, attic, or crawl space. We locate it, remove it, treat the odor, and disinfect the area. Same-day service available.

The Problem

A Dead Animal Won't Just "Go Away"

When an animal dies inside your home — in a wall, attic, or under the house — the smell gets worse every day. In Georgia's heat, decomposition happens fast. Within 24 to 48 hours, the odor can spread through your entire home.

Waiting it out doesn't work. The smell can last weeks or even months. Beyond the odor, a decomposing animal attracts flies, maggots, and other pests. It can also stain ceilings and walls as fluids seep through building materials.

Common locations we service:

  • 🏠 Inside walls
  • 📚 Attics & ceilings
  • 💧 Crawl spaces
  • 🌳 Yards & landscaping
  • 🚘 Under decks & porches
  • 🔧 HVAC ducts & vents
