Pests Don't Just Go Away

If you're seeing one roach, there are hundreds you're not seeing. Pests breed fast, spread disease, and cause damage. The sooner you act, the easier the problem is to solve.

Disease & Contamination

Rodents and roaches contaminate food, surfaces, and air quality. They spread salmonella, E. coli, and trigger asthma — especially in children.

Property Damage

Rats chew through wires, insulation, and drywall. Carpenter ants weaken wooden structures. The damage gets worse every week you wait.

They Multiply Fast

A single pair of mice can produce over 60 offspring in one year. Roaches reproduce even faster. What starts small becomes an infestation quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you use poison?
We avoid harsh poisons whenever possible. Our treatments use eco-friendly products and targeted methods that are effective against pests but safe for your family and pets. We focus on solving the root cause, not just masking the problem with chemicals.
Is your pest control safe for pets?
Yes, our pest control methods are pet-safe. We use products and techniques that target pests without putting your dogs, cats, or other pets at risk. We always let you know what we're applying and how to keep your pets comfortable during treatment.
How often do you need to treat?
It depends on the pest and the severity. Some problems are solved in a single visit. Others benefit from quarterly treatments to keep pests from coming back. We'll recommend the right schedule for your situation during the inspection.
What pests do you handle?
We handle rats, mice, roaches, ants, wasps, spiders, fleas, ticks, mosquitoes, and more. If it's a pest in Metro Atlanta, we've dealt with it. We tailor each treatment to the specific pest causing the problem in your home.
