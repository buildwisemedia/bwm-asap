The Problem

Pests Don't Just Bother You — They Put Your Health at Risk

A few ants in the kitchen or a mouse in the attic might seem small. But pests multiply fast. Roaches trigger asthma, especially in children. Rodent droppings carry bacteria like salmonella. And once pests settle into your walls or cabinets, store-bought sprays only push them deeper.

Many pest control companies drench your home in chemicals and hope for the best. That might knock down the visible bugs, but it doesn't fix the root cause. And those chemicals can be harmful to your family and pets.
