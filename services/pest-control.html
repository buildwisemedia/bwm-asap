Frequently Asked Questions

Is your pest control safe for kids and pets? +

Yes. We use eco-friendly products that are safe for children and pets. We'll let you know if any treated area needs a short drying time before re-entry.

How often do I need pest control treatments? +

Most homes benefit from quarterly treatments. Some pest problems like active infestations may need monthly visits at first. We'll recommend the right schedule during your inspection.

What pests do you treat? +

We treat rats, mice, roaches, ants, spiders, mosquitoes, wasps, termites, fleas, ticks, and most other household pests common to Metro Atlanta.

What can I do to prevent pests? +

Keep food sealed, fix water leaks, seal cracks around doors and windows, keep your yard trimmed, and store firewood away from your home. We'll give you specific tips during the inspection.

How much does pest control cost? +

Pest control typically costs between $150 and $800 depending on the type of pest, the size of your home, and the level of infestation. We provide a quote after a free inspection.
