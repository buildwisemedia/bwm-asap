Attic & Crawl Space Disinfection Done Right

Animal droppings, urine, and nesting material carry real health risks. We remove the contamination, disinfect the space, and make it safe for your family again.

The Problem

What Animals Leave Behind Can Make You Sick

After wildlife is removed, the mess they left is still a problem. Raccoon droppings can contain roundworm eggs that become airborne when disturbed. Bat guano can carry the fungus that causes histoplasmosis, a lung infection. Rodent urine and droppings are linked to hantavirus.

These aren't just unpleasant smells. They're real health hazards — especially if the contamination is in your attic, where air circulates into your living space. Cleaning it yourself without proper protection can make things worse by stirring up particles you then breathe in.

Health risks we address:

  • ⚠️ Raccoon roundworm (Baylisascaris)
  • ⚠️ Histoplasmosis (bat guano)
  • ⚠️ Hantavirus (rodent droppings)
  • ⚠️ Salmonella & E. coli
  • ⚠️ Parasites (fleas, ticks, mites)
  • ⚠️ Urine-soaked insulation
