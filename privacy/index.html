Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

ASAP Pest & Wildlife Removal (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you fill out a contact form, request a quote, or book a service, we collect the information you provide: name, phone number, email address, service address, and a description of your pest or wildlife issue.

When you visit our website, we automatically collect limited technical information through cookies and analytics tools: IP address, device type, browser, pages viewed, and referral source.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you give us to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule service
  • Provide wildlife removal, pest control, and related services
  • Send service confirmations, follow-ups, and appointment reminders
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Meet legal and tax obligations

SMS / Text Messages

If you opt in to receive text messages, we use your phone number to send service-related communications. You can opt out any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for help. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

We do not share mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers who help us run our business (scheduling, payment processing, email delivery) under contracts that require them to protect your information
  • Law enforcement or regulators when legally required

Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics (GA4), and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use our site. These tools may set cookies that record aggregated, anonymized usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

Your Rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Confirm what information we hold about you
  • Correct information that is wrong
  • Delete information we no longer need to keep
  • Stop receiving marketing communications

To make any of these requests, email info@removeasap.com or call (770) 450-1744.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy as our business changes or as required by law. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version.
